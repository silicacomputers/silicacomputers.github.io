About Us

Silica Computers started its operations in 2001 from Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh. From our humble beginnings in a small town to an organization with its central office in National Capital Region (NCR), our growth has been fueled by customer trust and loyalty which we believe has been a fallout of our ethical practices and great customer experience.

Our mission is to provide high-quality products and services at affordable prices. We strive to provide our customers with the best possible experience, from the moment they first contact us to the after sales commitment period. We believe in treating our customers with respect and honesty. We are proud to say that our customers have come to trust and rely on us, and we look forward to serving them for a very long period and be instrumental in their growth.
